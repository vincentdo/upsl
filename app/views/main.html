INFO

What

UPSL is the official League of Legends tournament sponsored by UPenn's student run League of Legends club. All registered players are drafted into teams of at least 6 members (5 starters and 1 or more subs) to compete all summer long.

Format

The format will be round-robin group stage qualifiers. The top teams from qualifiers will move on to the knock-out plays-off.
